# Privacy Policy for Last Tab Extension

**Last updated: January 23, 2026**

## Overview

Last Tab is a browser extension that helps you switch between tabs using keyboard shortcuts. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.**

The extension:
- Does NOT collect personal information
- Does NOT track your browsing history
- Does NOT send any data to external servers
- Does NOT use analytics or telemetry
- Does NOT share any information with third parties

## Data Storage

The extension stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

1. **Tab history** — A list of recently used tab IDs (not URLs or content) to enable MRU switching. This is stored in Chrome's local storage and cleared when you restart the browser.

2. **Settings** — Your preferences (enabled/disabled features) stored in Chrome's local storage.

This data never leaves your device.

## Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

- **tabs** — To switch between tabs and get tab information (title, favicon) for display in the popup
- **storage** — To save your settings locally
